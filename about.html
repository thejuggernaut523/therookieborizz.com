About us

Hello, I'm Wilson "Borizz" Paalaman and I'm on a journey to become the best barber I can be. While I may be new to the world of barbering, my passion for the craft and commitment to learning and improving is unwavering. Every haircut I create is a labor of love. I'm dedicated to honing my skills, and I treat each client's hair as a canvas for my growth.As a newcomer, I offer competitive prices for my services. It's a win-win – you get a quality haircut, and I get the opportunity to perfect my skills.

I may be a rookie, but I'm a good listener. I'm committed to understanding your preferences and delivering a haircut that suits your style. When you choose me as your barber, you're not just getting a haircut – you're supporting a budding talent. I'm here to learn and grow, and I promise to put my heart and soul into every haircut I deliver. Your feedback is invaluable in my journey to becoming a top-notch barber.I'm excited to be on this journey, and I'd love to have you as part of it. Feel free to reach out for an appointment or if you have any questions.
